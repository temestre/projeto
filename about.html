About me

I think I’ve been a designer since I can remember.

At that time - pre-school - my drawings were a bit advanced for my age (I even risk saying that I was better at hand drawing then).

Time passed, “arts” kept in my veins, and I enroled college to become a Designer.

Graphic, Interiors, Fashion, Web...I’ve done a bit of all by now, being Graphic my, let’s say...favorite.

But I always thought Design should go hand-in-hand with Marketing after all, who do we get the briefings from?

So I decided to learn how my Design impacted the public and actually understand the metrics and what they meant.

I turned to Digital Marketing.

During this time I learned how to manage corporate social media accounts, what was SEO, Ads, Analytics, keywords (really got into copywriting)...the whole shabang.

And there I was, fully digital, and then I thought to myself...

ow that I know my Digital and my Design, I have to go Digital Design!

And it got me here!

So now I’m hoping you give me the opportunity for fulfilling this goal.
