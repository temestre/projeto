Mobility Everywhere

Digital Design

Creating the complete trip concerns more than getting from A to B. The experience of the trip itself is important

All phases of travel must be taken into consideration. At each stage, different users may have different needs.

The research objetive was to understand how reduced physical mobility issues - wheelchairs, baby stollers, crutches, among others - interfere in the day-to-day life.
