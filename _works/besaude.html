Be Saúde

Stationary

BE Saúde is a dental and health franchise clinic.

While opening its second branch, it was in need of a small rebranding since the main colors were black and yellow.

By choosing light gray and different tones of blue the idea was to create an image easily relatable to medicine while transmiting some tranquility and confidence.

These are the stationary pieces and the opening promo flyer.
