Web Design | Ana Roque Interiors

A 23 years old luxury furniture brand decided to renew its single page website.

The idea was to make use of the great interior design images, presenting them full screen everytime possible.

Dividing the information into pages of interest to their target audience plus creating a blog and media page for dynamic content is the key for this successful portuguese company to showcase its work without leaving the country.
